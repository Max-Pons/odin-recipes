Curry Rice

Description

Curry rice is an easy side dish recipe, perfect for serving with your favorite
curries. A few pantry staples is all you need for this delicious dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 Garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp Ginger
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 cup Peas, frozen
  • 2 cups Jasmine / basmati rice
  • 2 tsp Curry powder / garam masala
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Turmeric
  • 4 cups Stock / water

Steps

  1. Saute onion in a medium-sized pot until translucent then add
    ginger, garlic and spices. I like using Garam Masala and
    Turmeric but you can use any Indian spices you have. Curry
    powder, Cardamom, Cumin, Coriander, etc. will all work well.
  2. Add the rice and a pinch of salt and stir to coat in the spices then pour
    in the water or stock. Allow the rice to cook until most of the liquid has been absorbed then stir in
    the peas (if using). Reduce the heat to its lowest setting, cover the rice and
    allow to cook for another 5 minutes. Turn off the heat and allow to steam for 10 minutes.
  3. Season the rice, fluff with a fork and serve.
